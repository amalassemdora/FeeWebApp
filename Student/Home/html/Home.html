About us

The Faculty of Electronic Engineering was formally designated and established in the city of Menouf in 1964. Menouf city lies to the south west of delta at about 65 km north of Cairo. It was established to be the lighthouse of Electronics in the Middle East. Being distinguished by this field made it the only exporter of engineers in the following sub-fields of electronics: Computer Science and Engineering. Automatic Control Engineering. Electronics and Electrical Communications Engineering. Until now, the Faculty of Electronic Engineering is the only specialized Faculty in this field in Egypt. Other universities may include only departments of Electrical and Electronic Engineering.
